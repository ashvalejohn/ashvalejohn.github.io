Ricardo Othon, PCCLT Homeowner

A home is more than a shelter.
It is a place to celebrate, to mourn, to heal, and to grow.

In seven short years, PCCLT has sold beautifully rehabilitated homes to 89 families, revitalized neighborhoods severely affected by the housing crisis, and stabilized families who never thought they would be able to purchase a home.

Please consider investing in our community by making a tax-deductible contribution to PCCLT today.

Betty Villegas, Board President

Maggie Amado-Tellez, Executive Director

Tax Information

PCCLT is a qualifying 501(c)(3) charity under the Arizona Tax Credit Program (Tax ID 27-2635994).

Taxpayers filing as “single” and “head of household” status may claim a maximum credit of $400. Taxpayers filing as “married filing separate” may claim a maximum credit of $400. Taxpayers that file as “married filing jointly” may claim a maximum credit of $800.

For more information please visit the Arizona Department of Revenue website.
